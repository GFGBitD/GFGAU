What is Among us?

Among Us is a party game for 4 10 PLAYERS. The players are divided into 2 teams, Crewmates and Impostors. As a Crewmate, your main gOAl will be to complete tasks to fill up the task gauge and the Crewmate team wins as soon as this gauge is filled, but you'll have to avoid getting killed by Impostors while doing this as the impostors aim to sabotage the entire mission before the task gauge fills up in the virtue of which they kill the crewmates. But while killing Crewmates, the impostors need to be careful not to be seen by other players, as they can and will eject you as an Imposter.
